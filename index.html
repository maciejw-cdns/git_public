About me and this blog (click to expand/collapse)

Hi friends!

My name is Maciek. I am not into any social media and this kind of stuff. I actually created this blog because of two main reasons:

  • Combining alternative view with out of the box thinking

    I could not find any blog presenting clear alternative view about the world we are living in with respect to the world we are shown by mainstream media combining with alternative articulated in simple non aggressive way. When we are coming across the alternative view of reality interestingly and reasonably described, we are also looking for solutions which must be really out of the box to make us believe not only in alternatives but also in possibility to realistically change the structure of the world. In my opinion most of the people doing similar research are quite aggressive and not necessarily objective trying to make the vague pieces of thesis to be undeniable facts. So even if we cannot accept being manipulated, we do not believe that the structure of the world can change as we do not see people having brilliant solutions which we can follow with real admiration. My goal is not to be the one who is supposed to lead the people to finally wake up and revolutionize the world order. I am just going to share some non aggressive ideas and visions which will hopefully motivate you to act…

  • Playing with Emacs

    Second reason is just playing with Emacs. I love working with plain text! Practicing Github is also useful to know.

I would like this blog to have additional value. Over and above its main goal, I am going to be adding interesting hints on various topics I like which can be interesting for people. Please expect some snippets about Craft beers, unique experiences from traveling, concept cars and vehicles (electric, self-driving, flying etc.) and high tech stuff - as I am an engineer…

I am not sure if the introduction above made you interested enough to check out this blog so I owe you revealing some information about myself.

As you may expect, I respect privacy very much and do not feel any need to share with the people what I had for breakfast or how many hours I slept in the last day.

But as I wrote, I feel that I owe you providing at least my personal profile data so let's have it done :)…

I am designing integrated circuits in large high tech company. I am interested in quantum computers and simulation (Matrix like) theory when it comes to technical stuff. Being eager to understand the structure of the word and being skilled to understand some relatively complex theories, the curiosity brought me to the point in which I made myself sure that the world is far different than most of the people think it is. There are many categories for this conclusion. Physical one and conceptual one. If motivated engineer (actually not only engineer) get to know that what they see is obviously wrong, they spend numerous hours finding answer. In some cases it is successful but in others - it cannot be, as there is no enough data to finally state anything as 'proved'. This is extremely frustrating and only thing which can be done to examine the topic very comprehensively is to have at least impressive background which other people can take advantage of…

That's it from me… Enjoy and help yourself thinking out of the box…

13/10/2018 David Icke is visiting Poland (click to expand/collapse)

David Icke is visiting my country (Poland) in one week. This is therefore good time to have closer look at that individual. You can find much information about the guy in the internet. As always, I am not going to copy easily accessible information into this post but just go into my opinion and outlook (about David Icke this time). Having said that, I would suggest to catch up about him before you continue reading. That would allow you to take advantage of the post more and have full understanding of what I am going to say regardless of what you think about this very controversial albeit unique and very interesting guy.

In a nutshell, Icke considers world structure far more different than one mainstream media inject into our brains. In theory, that ideally matches what I am looking for… But… Unfortunately, there is 'but'. I really appreciate people having own opinions and spending great deal of their time on further exploring. However, if I feel that something is not convincing enough, I cannot trust it and follow even if I want to.

One of the most popular Icke's thesis is about Reptilian extraterrestrial race governing the word. On the first glance, it seems the most ridiculous idea one has ever heard. I tried it, looking for arguments and was surprised not because I believed but rather because materials I found in favor of it just made me thinking. I did not expect it. That just was trigger for me to get deeper interest in David Icke.

I reached one of his books then and got disappointed… As I wrote, I appreciate all his effort in investigating things and following his believes but in many places in the book he defined some controversial theories as facts without necessarily arguments which could convince people with their minds drifting between starting to believe him or not. I was one of these people and in general I am going to stay interested in him but rather will continue to consider him as curiosity-sort-man - not as Messiah of any kind. He is very inspiring though so I recommend you reading his books to find some topics to explore. He is touching on many different things. So have distance but get inspired!
