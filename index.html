About me and this blog (click to expand)

Hi friends!

My name is Maciek. I am not into any social media and this kind of stuff. I actually created this blog because of two main reasons:

  • Combining alternative view with out of the box thinking

I could not find any blog presenting clear alternative view about the world we are living in with respect to the world we are shown by mainstream media combining with alternative articulated in simple non aggressive way. Having interestingly and reasonably described the alternative view of reality we are also looking for solutions which must be really out of the box to make us believe not only in alternatives but also in possibility to realistically change the structure of the world. In my opinion most of the people doing similar research are quite aggressive and not necessarily objective trying to make the vague theses to be undeniable facts. So even if we cannot accept being manipulated, we do not believe that the structure of world can change as we do not see people having brilliant solutions which we can follow with real admiration. My goal is not to be the one who is supposed to lead the people to finally wake up and revolutionize the world order. I am just going to share some non aggressive ideas and visions which will hopefully motivate you to act…

  • Playing with Emacs

Second reason is just playing with Emacs. I love working with plain text!

I would like this blog to have additional value. Over and above its main goal, I am going to be adding interesting hints on various topics which can be interesting for people. Please expect some snippets about Craft beers, unique experiences from traveling, and high tech stuff - as I am an engineer…

I am not sure if the introduction above made you interested enough to check out this blog so I owe you revealing some information about myself.

As you may expect, I respect privacy very much and do not feel any need to share what I had for breakfast or how many hours I slept the last day with the people.

But as I wrote, I feel that I owe you providing at least my profile so let have it done :)…

I am designing integrated circuits in big high tech company. I am interested in quantum computers and simulation (Matrix like) theory when it comes to technical stuff. Being eager to understand the structure of the word and being skilled to understand some relatively complex theories brought me to the point in which I made myself sure that the world is far different than most of the people think it is. There are many categories for this conclusion. Physical one and conceptual one. If motivated engineer (actually not only engineer) get to know that what they see is obviously wrong, they spend numerous hours finding answer. In some cases it is successful but in others - it cannot be as there is no enough data to finally state anything as 'proved'. This is extremely frustrating and only thing which can be done to examine the topic very comprehensively to have at least impressive background which other people can take advantage of…

That's it from me… Enjoy and help yourself thinking out of the box…
